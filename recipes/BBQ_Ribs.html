BBQ Ribs Recipe

How to make BBQ Ribs at Home!

Follow these simple steps so you can too enjoy this amazing meal

Do you like BBQ ribs that melt off the bone??? Well follow these steps and you can!

Ingredients

  • 2.5 pounds of country style pork ribs
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons salt
  • 1 cup of Sweet Baby Rays BBQ Sauce or one you like
  • 1 tablespoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Place ribs in large pot with water and bring to a boil. Cook ribs until tender
  2. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F
  3. Place ribs in oven and glaze with BBQ sauce and ingredients
