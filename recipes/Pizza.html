Making Pizza at Home!

Pizza too cheesy to resist!

Follow these instructions and you can have your own custom made Pizza!

Ingredients

  • dough
  • Cheese of choice
  • sauce of choice
  • meat toppings
  • vegetables
  1. Use the dough
  2. Place sauce on the dough
  3. Throw some cheese on top
  4. Slap on the meats and vegetables
  5. Preheat the oven to 325 F degrees
  6. Watch the pizza cook in 10 - 15 minutes
  8. ENJOY ITTTTTT THE PIZZA
